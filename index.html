Weplace is a neighborhood social commerce app for Malaysia combining community content and local marketplace workflows.

Both buyers and sellers can use Weplace. Seller mode includes products, orders, credits, and profile management.

The app currently supports English and Bahasa Malaysia, with localization architecture ready for expansion.

The first three product listings are free. Additional listings are charged at RM10 each.

Visit weplace.my/privacy, weplace.my/security, and weplace.my/tnc for policy details and support information.
