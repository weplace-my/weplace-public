Our Mission

Weplace is here to make your neighborhood friendlier, safer, and more connected. Join us and experience how easy it is to be part of a caring community!

The Power of a Local Marketplace

  • Zero Cost: No extra fees—neighbors can buy and sell without hidden charges.
  • Easy Delivery: Products are close by, so delivery is fast, simple, and often free.
  • For Everyone: Sell homemade goods, business products, or even in bulk—everyone is welcome.
  • Support Local Entrepreneurs: Weplace encourages people in your area to start and grow their own businesses.
  • Build Community: Buying and selling locally helps neighbors get to know each other and strengthens community bonds.

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Learn more about how Weplace works and how you can benefit from our neighbourhood marketplace.

Weplace connects you with sellers in your neighbourhood and nearby areas. You can choose to self-pickup from the seller's location or arrange for neighbour-to-neighbour delivery at no platform fees. Since sellers are within walking or short driving distance, there's no need for expensive courier services.

Absolutely! Weplace is designed for home-based entrepreneurs. Whether you bake from your home kitchen, craft handmade items, or grow organic vegetables, you can list your products and reach customers in your neighbourhood instantly. No rental, no overhead costs - just you, your products, and your neighbours.

Weplace shows you listings from your immediate neighbourhood and nearby neighbourhoods within a configurable radius (typically 3-5km). This ensures everything is within convenient pickup distance while giving you access to a wide variety of products and services from your local community.

Weplace charges a small commission (typically 5%) only when you make a sale. There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no hidden charges. You only pay when you earn, making it perfect for home-based businesses just starting out.

Yes! Weplace has a comprehensive rating and review system where buyers and sellers can rate each other. All users are verified, and we have built-in chat for transparent communication. Plus, since transactions happen locally within your community, there's an added layer of trust and accountability.
