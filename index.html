How Weplace Creates Value

Three ways Weplace brings neighborhoods to life.

Weplace connects people, shops, and opportunities within the same neighbourhood so daily life feels more useful, more affordable, and more connected.

Resident to Resident

Buy, sell, help, and connect directly with neighbors nearby. From homemade food to everyday support, residents can create value for one another, help reduce living costs, and support each other through rising prices and inflation.

Resident to Commercial

Local shops and verified sellers can offer vouchers, promotions, and better neighbourhood deals that feel relevant to the people around them.

Resident to Office

Open up business opportunities between residents, workplaces, and nearby demand with a platform designed for local discovery and real participation.

Built on Trust

Built for trusted local participation in Malaysia.

Trust matters in neighborhood communities. That is why Weplace is designed to support real local participation and clearer seller verification.

Verified account registration

Users can register as personal accounts by uploading IC front and back. Companies register with an SSM certificate. All submissions are verified within a short period.

Verified Malaysian sellers

Personal and business sellers can join if they are Malaysian citizens aged 18 and above. Sellers with SSM registration can show their business verification for added buyer confidence.

Safer local interactions

With local residents and verified Malaysian sellers, everyday buying, selling, and helping feel more trusted and secure.
