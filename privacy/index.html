Privacy

Your privacy matters.

Weplace is built to help Malaysian neighbourhoods connect, trade, and support one another. We only collect the information needed to run the platform safely and improve your experience.

What we collect

We may collect account and profile information such as your name, phone number, email address, profile image, and other information you choose to add to your account.

We may also collect home location and neighbourhood-related information so we can show relevant community content, nearby sellers, local events, market listings, and location-based features inside the app.

If you use messaging, reviews, reporting, help requests, or home/event posting features, we collect the content you submit, including text, uploaded images, and related timestamps or account references.

For sellers, we may collect seller registration and verification information, including business details, SSM-related information, authorised representative details, identity documents, selfie verification images, and optional halal-related documents where provided.

How we use your information

  • Provide platform features and account access
  • Support neighbourhood interactions, listings, and communication
  • Improve trust, safety, and service quality
  • Verify sellers, manage sales/orders, and support community commerce features
  • Send important updates, support responses, service alerts, and push notifications

Permissions we use

  • Location: Used to help users join or view relevant neighbourhood content, nearby listings, nearby sellers, and location-based community features.
  • Camera: Used when taking profile images, seller registration documents, identity verification photos, delivery proof, or other optional image uploads.
  • Photos / Files: Used when selecting images or files from your device for uploads such as product images, seller documents, chat images, and post attachments.
  • Notifications: Used to send push notifications such as chat activity, review alerts, seller/order updates, and other account or app-related notices.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the platform securely and reliably, such as hosting, database, notification, file storage, analytics, verification, or payment-related providers.

Information may also be disclosed where required by law, to protect users and the platform, or to investigate fraud, abuse, or safety issues.

Third-party services

Weplace may rely on third-party infrastructure and service providers to operate the app, such as cloud hosting, database services, push notification services, file storage services, maps/location-related tools, and payment providers.

Examples may include services used for mobile notifications, media/file hosting, backend infrastructure, and payment or top-up processing where applicable.

Data retention and deletion

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain safety and operational records, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

If you want to request account deletion or ask about your personal data, please contact us using the contact details below. Some records may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, financial, or operational reasons.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your information, including access controls, authentication, and infrastructure security measures. However, no internet-based service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

You may update certain account information inside the app. You may also contact us if you want to request deletion, correction, or clarification about how your personal data is handled.

Children

Weplace is intended for general community use and is not designed specifically for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, please contact us so we can review the request.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, contact us at weplace.my@gmail.com.
