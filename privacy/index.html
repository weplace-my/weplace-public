Privacy

Your privacy matters.

Weplace is built to help Malaysian neighbourhoods connect, trade, and support one another. We only collect the information needed to run the platform safely and improve your experience.

What we collect

We may collect basic account information such as your name, contact details, profile data, and information you provide while using the platform.

For residents, identity verification may be required to confirm Malaysian participation. For sellers, business verification details may be required, including SSM-related information.

How we use your information

  • Provide platform features and account access
  • Support neighbourhood interactions, listings, and communication
  • Improve trust, safety, and service quality
  • Communicate important updates, support information, and product changes

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate the platform securely and reliably.

Your choices

You may contact us at any time if you want to update or remove your account information, or if you have questions about how your data is handled.

Contact

If you have any privacy questions, contact us at weplace.my@gmail.com.
